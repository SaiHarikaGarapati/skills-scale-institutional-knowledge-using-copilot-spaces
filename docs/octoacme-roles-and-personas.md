# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

### Interactions
- Works with Product Managers on feature requirements and acceptance criteria
- Collaborates with Project Managers on timeline and blockers
- Partners with UX Designers to implement user-centered features
- Coordinates with QA/Testing on quality standards
- Supports Release Managers during deployment activities

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

### Interactions
- Partners with Developers on technical feasibility and estimation
- Works with UX Designers on user research and design validation
- Aligns with Data Analysts on success metrics and performance tracking
- Collaborates with Customer Support Liaison on user feedback and pain points
- Syncs with Project Managers on delivery timelines

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

### Interactions
- Coordinates with Developers on sprint planning and blockers
- Aligns with Product Managers on scope and priorities
- Works with Scrum Master to optimize team delivery flow
- Partners with Release Manager on deployment scheduling
- Receives insights from Data Analyst on team velocity and progress metrics

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers lead user research, create designs, and facilitate usability testing. They ensure that features are discoverable, intuitive, and meet user needs before implementation begins.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and design specifications
- Collaborate with developers on implementability and design refinement
- Advocate for user needs in product decisions
- Document design patterns and accessibility standards

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce design-to-implementation rework
- Build empathy for end-user needs across the team

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product and Development teams
- Usability testing reports and findings
- Design system documentation
- Accessibility audit results

### Interactions
- Partners with Product Managers on problem definition and user validation
- Collaborates closely with Developers during implementation to ensure design fidelity
- Works with Customer Support Liaison to understand real-world user pain points
- May work with Data Analyst to validate design effectiveness through user analytics
- Presents findings to stakeholders on user needs and design rationale

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate Agile ceremonies, coach the team on Agile best practices, and work to remove impediments that slow delivery. They act as a servant leader, enabling the team to self-organize and deliver incrementally.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups, sprint planning, retrospectives, and demos
- Coach the team on Agile methodologies and continuous improvement
- Identify and escalate blockers and impediments
- Protect the team from external distractions and scope creep
- Track sprint metrics and burndown
- Support retrospective action item follow-up

### Goals
- Maximize team velocity and consistency
- Create a psychologically safe environment for continuous learning
- Reduce friction in the delivery process

### Typical Communication
- Sprint ceremonies and standups
- Retrospective action items and tracking
- Blockers and impediments log
- Velocity and burndown charts

### Interactions
- Works closely with Project Managers to coordinate planning and track progress
- Supports Developers by removing impediments and facilitating collaboration
- Partners with Product Managers to refine backlog and acceptance criteria
- Escalates persistent blockers that require stakeholder or cross-team involvement
- Helps Data Analyst understand team capacity and metrics

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts define, track, and report on key performance indicators (KPIs) for product delivery and outcomes. They translate raw data into actionable insights that inform decision-making.

### Responsibilities
- Define and implement success metrics and KPIs
- Create dashboards and reports for team and stakeholder visibility
- Analyze product and delivery metrics to identify trends and opportunities
- Support A/B testing and feature validation
- Identify data quality issues and recommend improvements

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision-making across the organization
- Measure and demonstrate project and product impact
- Identify optimization opportunities through data insights

### Typical Communication
- Weekly or bi-weekly metric dashboards
- Analysis reports and insights presentations
- A/B test results and recommendations
- Data quality and methodology documentation

### Interactions
- Partners with Product Managers to define and track success metrics
- Provides insights to Project Managers on team velocity, project health, and delivery trends
- Works with Developers to instrument code for observability and testing
- Collaborates with UX Designers on user behavior analytics and design effectiveness
- Supports Scrum Master with team performance metrics
- Receives user insights from Customer Support Liaison to validate feature impact

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers coordinate the readiness, planning, and execution of software releases. They ensure that deployments are smooth, well-communicated, and have clear rollback plans.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain release checklists and runbooks
- Coordinate release timing across teams and environments
- Document changes and prepare release notes
- Verify pre-release requirements (testing, security, documentation)
- Coordinate deployment activities and post-deploy verification
- Lead rollback procedures if needed
- Communicate release status to stakeholders

### Goals
- Reduce deployment risk and lead time to production
- Ensure consistent, repeatable release processes
- Maintain transparency and reduce release anxiety

### Typical Communication
- Release readiness reports and checklists
- Release notes and change logs
- Deployment schedules and communications
- Post-deployment verification reports
- Incident postmortems and root cause analysis

### Interactions
- Coordinates with Developers to track code readiness and merge status
- Works with Project Managers on release scheduling and milestone planning
- Partners with QA/Testing to verify acceptance criteria and quality gates
- Collaborates with Customer Support Liaison on known issues and deployment communications
- Supports Data Analyst with release metadata for impact analysis
- May coordinate with Scrum Master on sprint-to-release cadence

---

## Customer Support Liaison

### Role Summary
Customer Support Liaisons serve as a bridge between customer-facing support teams and the product development team. They escalate user pain points, feedback, and insights that inform product improvements and priority adjustments.

### Responsibilities
- Escalate critical user issues and feature requests to product and engineering teams
- Analyze support ticket trends to identify systemic problems
- Provide user context and feedback to inform design and product decisions
- Assist in incident postmortems and root cause analysis
- Document known issues and workarounds for support team visibility
- Support release communications and training for support staff

### Goals
- Reduce time to resolution for customer issues
- Ensure product decisions are informed by real user feedback
- Improve customer satisfaction and reduce support volume through proactive product improvements

### Typical Communication
- Support ticket trends and escalation reports
- User feedback summaries and insights
- Known issues and workaround documentation
- Incident postmortems and lessons learned
- Release readiness briefings for support teams

### Interactions
- Works with Product Managers to prioritize issues based on customer impact
- Partners with Developers to provide user context and validate fixes
- Collaborates with UX Designers on user pain points and usability feedback
- Supports Release Managers by communicating known issues and deployment status to support teams
- Works with Data Analyst to track issue frequency and customer satisfaction metrics
- Escalates critical issues to Project Managers and stakeholders
